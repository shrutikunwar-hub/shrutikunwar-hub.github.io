The Challenge

BASF had no digital loyalty infrastructure for its 20M+ farmer customer base. Retention was driven purely by field relationships, with no scalable mechanism to reward repeat purchases or track behavior.

What I Built

I scoped, designed, and launched BASF's first digital loyalty program end-to-end — defining reward mechanics, designing 5 participation schemes, writing feature requirements, and creating a 5-year product roadmap.

The program grew downloads from 12K to 550K, grew MAU from ~2K to lakhs, and achieved 40% repeat participation across all 5 schemes in Year 1.

I also used SQL-based pattern detection to identify a structural loophole in reward mechanics, recommending a fix that saved $50,000 annually.
