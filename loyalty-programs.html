01 / Story
The Challenge

No infrastructure. No scalable reward loop.

BASF had no digital loyalty infrastructure for its farmer customer base. Repeat purchase behavior existed in the field, but there was no structured system to reward it, track it, or scale it.

BaselineOffline retention
What I Built

An end-to-end loyalty system with clear ownership.

I scoped, designed, and launched the program end to end — defining reward mechanics, designing five participation schemes, writing feature requirements, and building a five-year product roadmap.

BuildMechanics + roadmap
Business Impact

Adoption growth with better economics.

The program helped take downloads from 12K to 550K, achieved 40% repeat participation, and surfaced a structural reward loophole that I flagged and fixed through SQL-based pattern detection.

Outcome$50K protected annually
